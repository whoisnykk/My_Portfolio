Job Insight

Job Insight is a smart web platform built to help students prepare effectively for placements by providing the most commonly asked questions in aptitude, coding, and interview rounds — specific to top companies like TCS, Infosys, and Wipro. Instead of searching multiple platforms, users simply select a company and instantly access practice questions seen in real test rounds. This ensures focused, time-saving, and company-targeted preparation.

BloodConnect System

A web-based platform that connects blood donors with individuals in need by allowing users to register with their blood group, contact, and location details. When someone requires blood, they can easily search for compatible donors in their area and contact them directly through the platform.
